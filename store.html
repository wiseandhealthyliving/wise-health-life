Apothecary Safety & Storage

Storage Rules

  • Cool & Dark: Store in a cupboard away from the stove or sunlight.
  • Airtight: Use glass jars with tight lids to prevent oxidation.
  • Labeling: Always mark the date of purchase and the common/Latin name.

Safety First

  • Check for interactions if you are on prescription medication.
  • Verify "Pregnancy Safe" status for each herb before use.
  • Discard any herbs that show signs of moisture or mold.
