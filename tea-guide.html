The Art of the Infusion

Temperature and timing are the keys to unlocking herbal potency.

Infusion

Best for leaves and flowers. Pour hot water over the herbs and cover to keep volatile oils from escaping.

Decoction

Best for roots and bark. Simmer the herbs in boiling water for 15–20 minutes to extract minerals.

Herb Type Temp Time Best For
Delicate Flowers
Chamomile, Lavender		 85°C 3–5 mins Relaxation, Sleep
Medicinal Leaves
Peppermint, Moringa		 95°C 5–10 mins Digestion, Energy
Fresh Roots
Ginger, Turmeric		 100°C 10–15 mins Nausea, Joints
Dried Bark/Roots
Dandelion, Cinnamon		 100°C+ 20 mins Detox, Immunity
Wise Tip: Always cover your cup while steeping! The steam contains the "volatile oils"—the very part of the herb that provides the aroma and many medicinal benefits.
